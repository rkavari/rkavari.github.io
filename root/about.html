About

Muddy Paws Adventure Gear was created to address the lack of stylish yet comfortable traveling solutions available for our best friends.

Created here in Pittsburgh by a pet-loving family, our designs are made in house and first tried on by our beloved pets before we share them to yours. Our gear is made with the utmost love and care.

You can join the Muddy Paws Adventure Gear family today by getting your furry best friend the best gear available on the market today. With our gear, they will be ready to take on adventure one paw at a time. Nothing is a challenge for them when they are geared up with Muddy Paws.
